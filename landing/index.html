WE ARE IMMER


Your pet's health records - any pet, any vet, always yours


More

Any pet, any vet

Our records management platform is designed for pets and their people

A blockchain registry ensures that you always have all of your pet's records.

With blockchain, your pet's records are embedded in a cloud database where they are protected from tampering and deletion. Each record is encrypted and signed with a secure digital signature that validates its authenticity.

Ultra-reliable cloud storage means that your pet's records are always available, whenever you need them.

24 x 7 backed up cloud storage with 0% fault tolerance provides peace of mind when it comes to accessing your pet's health data, anywhere, anytime.

Advanced veterinary analytics help your pet receive the most up to date and highest quality of care.

Immer's advanced analytics, powered by machine learning, analyze your pet's health data and epidemiological environment to recommend the care options best suited to your pet.

An intuitive voice assistant schedules veterinary appointments for you whenever your pet needs one.

Just pick up your phone, invoke Siri, Google Home or Alexa, and say "Open Immer. I need to schedule an appointment." Immer's voice assistant will respond with scheduling options and confirm your pet's appointment.

Our platform guarantees that your pet's records are portable anywhere.

Whenever you switch to a new veterinarian, Immer will securely port your records to your pet's new care giver. You can download your pet's records for your own use at any time.

Immer is always free to pet owners!

At Immer, we believe that when it comes to managing your pet's health, you should have access to state of the art technology at no cost.

Company History

Founded by Four Harvard Extension School Graduate Students

Immer originated from ISMT E-599, Harvard Extension School's graduate capstone seminar in digital enterprise. The company concept was inspired by a shared interest in novel blockchain applications and advanced analytics, especially machine learning - as well as our pets!
